Weighbridge Capture Integration for Acumatica

Direct weighbridge serial-port / TCP capture integration that streams gross, tare and net weights into Acumatica purchase receipts and stock movements.

Overview

Paper weight tickets and manual data entry are the #1 source of inventory variance for bulk-goods businesses. This integration connects weighbridge indicators (Mettler Toledo, Avery, Rice Lake, generic RS232/TCP) directly to Acumatica, so the weight on the scale is the weight on the purchase receipt — full stop. The operator confirms; Acumatica posts.

Core Features

  • Serial (RS232/485) & TCP weighbridge support
  • Gross / tare / net capture in a single operator screen
  • Auto-creation of Acumatica Purchase Receipts & Inventory Receipts
  • Vehicle registration, driver & supplier lookup
  • Two-pass (inbound / outbound) weighing workflow
  • Weight ticket PDF with photo & signature capture
  • GL posting & costing using Acumatica standard flow
  • Audit trail of raw indicator stream for compliance

How It Works

1. Connect
Local agent reads the weighbridge indicator over serial or TCP.
2. Capture
Operator confirms gross/tare; net is computed and validated.
3. Post
A Purchase Receipt (or Inventory Transaction) is created in Acumatica.
4. Attach
Weight ticket PDF, signatures, and photos are attached to the receipt.
5. Ship/Invoice
Downstream Acumatica processes pick up the receipt unchanged.

Best Fit

  • Agribusiness, feed mills, cement, scrap metal, aggregates, FMCG
  • Companies automating purchase receipts from bulk deliveries
  • Multi-site operations needing centralized weight capture data
