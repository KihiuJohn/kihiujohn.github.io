How we work together

Three engagement shapes covering most projects. Hybrids are common — feel free to ask which fits your situation best.

Fixed-scope project

Best when you know exactly what you need. We agree the scope, deliverables, milestones, and total price upfront. Changes go through a written variation order.

Rate: USD 40 / hour
Timeline: 4–16 weeks typical
Payment: 40% / 40% / 20% milestones

Time & materials

Best when scope is exploratory or evolving. You buy a block of hours, I bill weekly with a detailed time log. Easy to pause, resume, or extend.

Rate: USD 40 / hour
Min block: 40 hours

Monthly retainer

Best for live systems that need ongoing love. Fixed monthly fee for a defined capacity, plus guaranteed response times for incidents.

Capacity: 20–80 hours / month
Commitment: 3 months minimum
