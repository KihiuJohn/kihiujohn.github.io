Acumatica Service Management — Case Lifecycle

A walk through the Acumatica Service Management case lifecycle — from creation to resolution, with the SLA, escalation, and billing rules that turn support into a tracked process.

John KihiuSep 25, 20256 min read

Service Management is Acumatica's help-desk and customer support layer. Cases are the central document. They move through a lifecycle, accrue time, and (if billable) feed into an AR invoice.

The lifecycle

  • Open — newly created, not yet assigned.
  • In Progress — assigned, work in flight.
  • Pending — waiting on the customer or a third party.
  • Resolved — the fix is in; waiting for customer confirmation.
  • Closed — final.

SLAs

Configure SLAs per case class and priority. Acumatica monitors and escalates when an SLA is at risk or breached. Email notifications keep the assignee and the customer informed.

Wrapping up

That is the working approach I use on Acumatica projects. The same patterns show up whether you are in Nairobi, Johannesburg, Kigali, Lusaka or Harare — and they are the things that keep work moving when an upgrade lands at 6 PM on a Friday.
